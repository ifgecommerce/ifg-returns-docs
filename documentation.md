## Overview

IFG Returns & Withdrawals handles product returns and the EU 14-day right of withdrawal end to end: a customizable storefront return form, an admin dashboard to review and process cases, refund handling, and status tracking — with the legal text, languages, and workflow needed to run this in a way that matches EU consumer-rights rules.

## EU withdrawal compliance

The storefront form and its legal text are built around the EU right of withdrawal (Directive 2011/83/EU) and the withdrawal-function requirements added by Directive (EU) 2023/2673, effective 19 June 2026. In practical terms, the app:

- Shows a clear, always-available electronic withdrawal function on your storefront — no phone call or manual email required.
- Verifies the order live against Shopify before accepting a request, and gives the customer an immediate acknowledgment of receipt.
- Tracks whether your store's legal details and return address are filled in, since both are required for a compliant withdrawal notice.

This is a description of how the app behaves, not legal advice — merchants remain responsible for their own compliance obligations.

## Data & privacy

Access uses Shopify's expiring offline access tokens, refreshed automatically. When the app is uninstalled (or a data-erasure request is received), customer personal data — name, email, phone, and free-text notes — is anonymized. Order-level accounting fields (order number, resolution, amounts) are kept, anonymized, since they're required accounting records under standard fiscal retention rules (up to 10 years).
