Billed in USD through Shopify Billing. Annual billing on Grow and Unlimited is roughly two months free compared to paying monthly. Switch plans or cancel any time from **Plans** inside the app — changes take effect immediately, no email required.

## Free — $0/month

- Storefront return & withdrawal form, in all 30 interface languages (full right-to-left layout for Arabic and Hebrew)
- Guided onboarding (company legal details + return address)
- Admin case review workflow: approve, confirm receipt, track status
- Live order verification against Shopify before a request is accepted
- EU 14-day right-of-withdrawal compliance built in

## Grow — $4.99/month ($49.90/year)

Everything in Free, plus:

- Per-item return reasons
- Direct in-app refunds (approve → confirm receipt → refund)
- PDF receipts, with removable IFG branding
- Downloadable summary reports
- Analytics page
- CSV export

## Unlimited — $9.99/month ($99.90/year)

Everything in Grow, plus:

- Let the customer choose their resolution: **refund, discount voucher, or item exchange**
- Product Restrictions (exclude specific products or categories from automatic returns)

## Billing questions

**Is this a test charge on a development store?**
Yes — on a Shopify development store, subscribing to a paid plan creates a Shopify *test charge*. No real billing occurs until the app runs on a live store.

**Do prices ever change without notice?**
No. Shopify's Billing API requires merchant approval for any plan change before it takes effect.

**Can I switch or cancel any time?**
Yes — from **Plans** inside the app. Changes take effect immediately, with no email required.
